## Diagnostic Perspective

*Based on a holistic view, focus on the following connections:*

1. **"Spirit" (Shen) & "Form" (Xing)**:
   - *Principle*: When the spirit is unsettled, the form is restless. Hyperactivity is a manifestation of "spirit" having nowhere to settle.
   - *Focus*: Eye expression (scattered/dull/alert), sleep quality (night terrors/teeth grinding/night sweats).

2. **Bowel Movements & Organ Systems**:
   - *Principle*: The large intestine is the organ of transmission; bowel patterns reflect the rise and fall of qi.
   - *Focus*: Constipation (gastrointestinal heat accumulation/yin-deficient dry intestines), diarrhea (spleen deficiency with dampness).

3. **Cold-Heat & Deficiency-Excess**:
   - *Focus*: Sensitive to cold or heat? Prefers cold or warm drinks? Hot or cold palms? Where does sweating occur (head/hands-feet/back)?
   - *Tongue (if user can provide)*: Red tongue (heat), pale tongue (deficiency), thick greasy coating (dampness/food stagnation), geographic tongue (yin deficiency).
   - *Skin/Hair*: Dry skin/peeling (blood deficiency with wind-dryness/yin deficiency), brittle nails/white spots (liver blood insufficiency), sallow complexion (spleen deficiency).

## Common Pattern Types (Suggestive)

1. **Kidney Yin Deficiency / Deficiency Fire Rising**:
   - Manifestations: Thin build, hot palms and soles, irritability, hyperactivity, red tongue with little coating, dry skin, night sweats.
   - Treatment: Nourish yin and reduce fire (e.g., black beans, black sesame, Chinese yam).

2. **Heart-Spleen Deficiency**:
   - Manifestations: Scattered attention (frequent daydreaming), mental fatigue, sallow complexion, poor appetite, light sleep, pale nails.
   - Treatment: Strengthen spleen and nourish heart (e.g., longan, red dates, poria).

3. **Liver Yang Rising**:
   - Manifestations: Hot temper, impulsivity, shouting, red eyes.
   - Treatment: Calm liver and subdue yang (e.g., celery, chrysanthemum).

## Treatment Modalities

- **Pediatric Massage**: Spine pinching (regulate yin-yang), rubbing Yongquan point (guide fire downward), clearing Tianhe water (clear heat).
- **Diet**: Align with seasonal changes, avoid rich, greasy foods that promote dampness and heat.
