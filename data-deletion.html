Your Right to Data Deletion

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the right to request the deletion of your personal data. This page provides information about how to exercise this right and what data will be deleted.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request data deletion, we will remove the following information:

  • Your email address and contact information
  • Your waitlist registration data
  • Your social media profile information (if connected)
  • Your communication preferences
  • Your account activity logs
  • Any other personal information associated with your account

We will respond to your data deletion request within 30 days of receipt. In some cases, we may need additional time to process your request, but we will notify you if this is necessary.

Data Retention Exceptions

  • Financial records (if applicable)
  • Legal compliance requirements
  • Security and fraud prevention
  • Dispute resolution
